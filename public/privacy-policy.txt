# Privacy Policy

Tunela VPN may process limited technical data required to provide VPN connection, diagnostics, and app stability monitoring.

## Data categories

- Technical diagnostics (errors, crashes, runtime status)
- Connection metadata required for app functionality
- Device/app version metadata required for compatibility and support

## Purpose of processing

- Provide VPN functionality
- Maintain service stability and reliability
- Investigate and fix technical issues
- Improve app quality and user experience

## Third-party services

Tunela may use third-party services for analytics, diagnostics, and update infrastructure as part of app operation.

## Security

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are applied to protect processed data.
